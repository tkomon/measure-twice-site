Measure Twice — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Measure Twice is a material calculator for DIY renovation projects. It is designed to work entirely on your device.

What we store

All of your data — calculations, saved projects, shopping lists, and settings — is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers, and your data is never uploaded, synced, or shared. The app requires no account and works fully offline.

Purchases

In-app purchases (the Pro unlock and optional tips) are processed by Google Play. We use RevenueCat, a purchase-management service, to validate purchases and restore them on new devices. Neither we nor RevenueCat receive your name, email address, or payment details; purchase records are identified only by an anonymous identifier.

Analytics

We collect anonymous usage analytics (for example: which calculators are opened and which buttons are tapped) via PostHog to understand which features are useful and to improve the app. This data contains no personal information, no account identifiers, and no precise location. It cannot be used to identify you.

What we do not do

We do not collect, sell, or share personal data. We do not show ads. We do not request any device permissions.

Children

Measure Twice is a general-audience utility and does not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new "last updated" date.
